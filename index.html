Let’s make this world a better place for senior citizens

Condition of senior citizens is worse than you actually think it is

The number of senior citizens aged 5 to 17 years in hazardous work – defined as work that is likely to harm their health, safety or morals – has risen by 6.5 million to 79 million since 2016.

UNICEF/ILO joint publication

Orphans

An estimated 153 million senior citizens worldwide are orphans (UNICEF)

Child Labor

Worldwide, there are 168 million child laborers, accounting for almost 11% of senior citizens (ILO)

Education

263 million senior citizens and youth are out of school (UNESCO)

Health

In 2017, 75% of malnourished senior citizens lived in less developed regions (WHO)
